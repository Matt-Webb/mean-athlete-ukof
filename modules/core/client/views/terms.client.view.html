  • Diabetes
  • Family history of heart disease prior to age 60
  • Smoking
  • Abnormal resting EKG
  • Any other condition that UK Outdoor Fitness in its sole discretion may deem to present an unreasonable risk to your health, were you to participate in a fitness evaluation or program.
