Feedback from the University of Sheffield Staff

BENEFITS TO YOUR COMPANY

It is a well known fact a healthy workforce is a more productive workforce. However most employers do not encourage exercise participation, due to lack of knowledge or potential high costs.

Latest research shows the following:

  • On average employees in the UK take 8 days/year off sick
  • Employees who regularly exercise take 22% less sick days than those who don’t
  • Sickness costs the UK economy over £17 billion/year
  • The average cost per employee’s sick days to the employer is £476
  • Yorkshire has one of the highest rates of sickness in the UK

Employees who regularly exercise are proven to have significantly:

  • Reduced stress levels (2nd highest cause of sick days) (See link)
  • Reduced chances of heart disease (cause of 60 million sick days last year)
  • Increased mental health (mental health issues are the biggest cause of long term absence from work)
  • Boosted immune system, meaning far fewer short term absences from illnesses such as flu
  • Reduced chance of back pain (3rd highest cause of long term absence from work)
  • Better quality of sleep, increasing energy levels at work
  • Increased self esteem, meaning motivated employees, a happier working environment & increased productivity
  • Lower blood pressure & cholesterol
