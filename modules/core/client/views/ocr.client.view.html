Don’t worry if you are a novice to Obstacle course races / mud runs. We help you from start to finish. From beginners to advanced levels. You can train up for the races through attending our classes around Yorkshire. Please check out the locations we train at: http://www.ukoutdoorfitness.com/classes/

Our expert trainers can tell you want to expect, offer advice and actually race with you when we go to the event!
